# Basics

## Breakpoints

Breakpoints provide a way to organize your styles by specif screen sizes.

Particle has 4 breakpoints: Small, medium, large and very large:

| Breakpoint | Alias | Min size |
|------------|-------|----------|
| Small      | s     | 480px    |
| Medium     | m     | 768px    |
| Large      | l     | 1024px   |
| Very large | vl    | 1200px   |
